## Problem

Enterprise AI at scale needs more than a good model. Teams were building one-off
LLM solutions with no shared standards for agents, prompts, deployment, or
observability — slow to ship and hard to govern in an FDA- and HIPAA-regulated
environment.

## What I built

I architected the Merck AI Agentic Framework: a reusable operating model for
multi-agent LLM applications. It standardizes agent patterns, prompt-engineering
practices, MLOps pipelines, and observability so teams ship production AI on a
common, governed foundation rather than reinventing it each time.

## Outcome

Adopted across the CTO division as the backbone for enterprise AI delivery,
impacting 36+ end-to-end processes with realized improvements ranging from 26%
to 315%+.
