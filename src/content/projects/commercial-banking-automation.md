## Problem

Front-office Commercial Banking and Lending operations were heavy with manual,
repeatable work — expensive, slow, and hard to scale.

## What I built

I architected enterprise automation across Commercial Banking and Lending and
spearheaded adoption of intelligent automation (RPA, OCR, APIs, AI/NLP) by
influencing C-suite stakeholders and embedding automation into the front-office
operating model, backed by multi-year roadmaps, OKRs, and KPI dashboards.

## Outcome

Delivered $200M+ in cumulative operational cost savings across Lending and
Treasury Management.
