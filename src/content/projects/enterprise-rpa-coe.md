## Problem

The bank had no shared platform, patterns, or standards for robotic process
automation — every line of business was on its own, and compliance-critical
alerting needed to be faster and more accurate.

## What I built

I founded the enterprise RPA Center of Excellence as a 0-to-1 build:
co-authoring the RPA architecture, design patterns, and engineering standards;
standing up platforms across Dev, UAT, and Production; and authoring a reusable
Automation Code Library. I led onshore and offshore teams automating data
collection for AML and fraud account alerting.

## Outcome

Increased production solution output 48%, improved fraud/AML detection accuracy
35%, and strengthened the bank's regulatory posture.
