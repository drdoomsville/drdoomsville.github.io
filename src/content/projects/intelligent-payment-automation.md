## Problem

High-volume payment processing demanded fault tolerance and throughput that
manual and brittle workflows could not sustain, with revenue leaking through the
gaps.

## What I built

I architected cloud-based intelligent automation on Azure — leveraging Python,
C#, Java, and Blue Prism — for fault-tolerant, high-throughput payment
processing, and transformed the teams behind it into high-performing Agile units
with CI/CD, peer review, and automated testing.

## Outcome

Supported 50M+ financial transactions per year, reduced revenue leakage 15%+, and
doubled solution delivery throughput.
