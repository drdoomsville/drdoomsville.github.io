## Problem

Fortune 500 clients across financial services, public sector, telecom, and
healthcare needed faster case processing and real-time visibility into
high-volume, multi-system workflows.

## What I built

I designed and deployed Pega Case Management and Robotic Automation solutions —
engineering Provider and Consumer bots, real-time Robot Dashboard and Health
Status portals, and end-to-end case orchestration. Notable deliveries included
CIBC's customer onboarding (Compass), Blue Cross Blue Shield robotic automation,
the US Census "Census" framework, and T-Mobile repair vendor management.

## Outcome

Reduced case processing times by 67% and scaled CIBC onboarding to 1,200+
branches.
