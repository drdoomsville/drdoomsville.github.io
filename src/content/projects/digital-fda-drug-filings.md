## Problem

Regulatory submissions and clinical study processing were slow, manual, and
document-heavy — a bottleneck on getting work to the FDA.

## What I built

I delivered LLM-powered automation of regulatory submissions: extracting,
assembling, and validating the documentation behind clinical study filings, with
the governance and observability to run safely in a regulated context.

## Outcome

Cut clinical study processing times by 30%+ and laid a scalable foundation
projected to reduce processing time by 60%+.
