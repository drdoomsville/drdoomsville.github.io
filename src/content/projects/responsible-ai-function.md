## Problem

As AI adoption accelerated, the enterprise faced mounting regulatory exposure
with no consistent way to assess, approve, or govern AI solutions against
emerging global standards.

## What I built

I founded and operationalized Merck's Responsible AI function — evangelizing and
embedding enterprise-wide policies aligned with the EU AI Act and NIST AI RMF. I
mandated AI Assessments on 100% of AI solutions, building the assessment program,
review gates, and governance frameworks that made compliance a default rather
than an afterthought.

## Outcome

Reduced enterprise compliance exposure by 70%+ while keeping AI delivery moving,
with FDA, HIPAA, and emerging AI-regulation requirements covered by design.
